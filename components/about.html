About Us

Empowering people to take control of their finances through clarity, simplicity, and design.

Empowering Smart Financial Management

At SpendList, our mission is to empower users to take control of their finances through a clean, intuitive, and reliable digital platform. We aim to provide an accessible tool that helps individuals organize and track their income, expenses, and budgets — making personal finance simple, visual, and stress-free.

Our Vision

SpendList was born from the need for a minimalist, data-driven platform that transforms how people understand and manage their money. Our application allows users to register and manage transactions, view budget overviews, and gain insights through interactive reports. By combining elegant design with robust architecture, SpendList delivers both beauty and functionality.

Technical Foundation

The platform integrates a structured database capable of storing, querying, and updating financial data dynamically. Our system architecture balances performance, scalability, and simplicity, while the UX design ensures clarity and visual harmony for decision-making.

User-Centered Research

Before development, we conducted a probabilistic user study to understand the most common financial challenges, such as tracking expenses, categorizing spending, and visualizing budgets. These insights helped shape SpendList into a truly user-focused platform, complete with visual charts and smart categorization.

Our Core Objectives

  • General Objective: Build a website that helps users manage their capital effectively, gaining control over expenses, income, and other financial activities.
  • Specific Objectives:
    • Design an appealing, user-friendly web interface.
    • Understand and apply the technologies used in its creation.
    • Promote financial literacy through clarity and accessibility.
